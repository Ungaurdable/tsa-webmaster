About Us

Hello everyone! Hafiza means memories. Eating at Hafiza will take you back to your childhood, sitting at the dinner table, eating with your family. Hafiza will take you back to . Hafiza is a Turksih family-owned and run restaurant in Boone, Carolina. We are a mediterranean-style restaurant with with two michelin stars; we are the top of the top! We migrated from Greece in the 2000s, where we used to have a food stall in the mountains. We came to Boone, North Carolina because we wanted the same cozy, homey feeling we got up in the mountains in Greece.
